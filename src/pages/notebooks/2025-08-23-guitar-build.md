The primary goal for this build was to be able to separate each of the six strings of the 
electric guitar into a discrete signal for digitization, computational processing, and 
rendering an analog output to a small [Bugara T5 INFINIUM](https://www.bugera-amps.com/product.html?modelCode=0710-AEO) 
tube amplifier for auditorialization. A secondary goal of the build was to afford the maximum 
amount of variability of function and tone for any transducer installed into the body. 
Unsurprisingly, investigating the smaller builders and shops proved to inspire.

![Lace Alumitone Humbucker Dimensional Layout](/notebooks/lace-alumitone-dimensions.png)

I decided to installing a Lace Alumitone humbucker as the primary magnetic pickup. Maximum 
variability being the secondary goal, I considering my coil splitting options: (1) use a mini 
toggle for clean separation of single-coil and humbucking modes, (2) introduce a potentiometer 
to gracefully to attenuate a coil, or (3) something I haven't envisioned (asked for suggestions 
on social media, received nothing).
