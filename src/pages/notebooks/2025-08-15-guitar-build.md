Used a Dremel router to create the pickup cavity and surface notch to hold the metallic 
pickguard. I'll coat the interiors with a layer of MG Chemicals carbon conductive paint 
to help mitigate radio frequency interference after the electronics are situated. Next up, 
shaping and preparing the steel pickguard.
