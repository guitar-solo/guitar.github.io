Nu modules are single-string, active pickups utilizing low-impedance coils and an integrated 
low-power, low-noise preamplifier to drive the signal. Although the modules can be purchases
individually, the Nu Multi 6 is designed to be retrofitted into the standard single-coil pickup
slot of any pickguard to provide hexaphonic signal outputs. The frequency response is nearly
uniform through the 20Hz to 20Khz spectrum.
