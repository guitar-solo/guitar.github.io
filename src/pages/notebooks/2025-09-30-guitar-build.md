All termination points of electrical wiring have been soldered and wrapped in heat shrink
tubing for stress control. Double-sided foam tape was used to secure the Graph Tech and
Cycfi internal breakout board to backside of the metallic pickguard.

![stress relief on switch wiring](/notebooks/wire-stress-relief.jpg)

The initial plan was to secure the two 250k potentiometers to the backside of the
plastic body cavity cover. 3M heavy duty, double-sided foam tape was strong enough to
adequately secure the controls without any issue. Upon a second evaluation, the inverted
placement ensured the controls would remain out-of-the-way and easily accessible; but,
removing the backplate would be problematic and potentially destructive without care.

![internal body cavity with potentiometers on top](/notebooks/potentiometer-on-top.jpg)

Moving the potentiometers to the backside of the metallic pickguard became the selected
option. The main electronics cavity is slightly cramped, but I think it's worth reducing
accidental wire separation or unnecessary strain on the cabling when opening the backplate. 

![internal body cavity with potentiometers on bottom](/notebooks/potentiometer-on-bottom.jpg)
