## PolyMap

David Wieland of [Dark Arts Guitars](https://darkartguitars.com/)

This guitar, featuring the [PolyMap Pickup System](https://darkartguitars.com/blog/polymap), is my Master's Thesis in Electrical Engineering at [ETH Zürich](https://ethz.ch/en.html). 
Not only are there 64 pickups, but they are all digitized inside the guitar and can be simultaniously recorded. 
To choose which pickups are active, there is a custom plugin that also incorporates various effects.
